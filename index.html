Our Services

The Best Daycare

Play n Learn Child Care Home is licensed by the Manitoba Early Learning and Child Care Program. We abide by all regulations as set out in the Best Practices Licensing Manual for Early Learning and Child Care Centers and we are subject to annual licensing visits from our government coordinator.

Reduced Occupancy due to COVID

We have a small group of 8 children which is easy to handle midst of the pandemic, as small groups less exposure.

Infants

We are licensed for 3 infants ages from 3 weeks to 2 years. Keeping the fact in the mind that infants develop at their best in a happy and caring environment.

Pre School

We are licensed for 2 preschool ages 2 to 5 years. Where they are provided the environment where they get the chances of brain development to full extent and learn in play like method.

School Age

As per license we have 3 school age spots where children feel the home like comfort before and after school.

Visit Our Daycare

Play n Learn Child Care Home offers a safe, accessible and affordable childcare program for children ages from 3months to 12years of age. Our hours of operation are from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday with the maximum number of 8 Children

About us

Our Philosophy

We believe that children can learn and grow when provided with an exciting environment and we provide warm and caring individual to offer guidance, as well as a creative program within set limits. Its being observed by the experience that every child is a complete personality and has the potential. Everyday they learn, just the pace of learning is different, which show our diversity as human beings. We encourage age appropriate, non-sexist, non-competitive and creative programming allowing children freedom of choice and an opportunity to learn independence, responsibility and develop a positive self-concept. We believe in mutual respect and each child is treated as a respected individual. Parents/Guardians are the most important people in children’s lives; as such they are highly encouraged to spend quality time with their children both at home and in Our Child Care Facility.

We recognize the importance of multi cultural community in Canada so do we encourage of celebration of ethnic, religious, and cultural festivals/birthdays and would love to celebrate and learn about different festivals by encouraging parents to share their traditions with us.

infrastructure

Ample in-door Sun room with spacious out door play space to enjoy the weather when permits.

Qualified Staff

Our Directors, Substitutes and Childcare Assistants are certified ECE III and First aid CPR infants, children and adults respectively.

Small Class Size

Due to COVID-19 we have reduced the occupancy to adhere to Government policies.

Infant Care

Infants are provided age appropriate and washable toys, comfortable and cozy nap area for them where they go to their dream land happily.

Safety First

All of our staff is COVID-19 Vaccinated and all the playareas are hazard free. Facility and all the toys are sanitized by the end of everyday and all the safety measures by public health are followed.

Activities

Free play, teacher directed activity, dramatic play, art and craft and Outdoor play

Meet our Qualified Staff

The director has a current job description, including all competency areas – safety, health, learning environment, physical, cognitive, communication, creative, self, social, guidance, families, program management and professionalism. The director participates in at least 24 hours of professional development in early childhood education each year. Professional development is any opportunity to learn new skills, enhance existing skills or learn information that builds competence, self-confidence, personal empowerment or a support network. It includes a range of activities such as home visits, written resource materials, mentoring, workshops and certificate courses.
